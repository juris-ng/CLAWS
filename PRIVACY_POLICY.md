# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: October 11, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to Civic Voice ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number (optional)
- **Profile Information**: Avatar, bio, location (optional)
- **Content**: Petitions, comments, votes you create

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app
- **Device Information**: Device type, OS version, unique device identifiers
- **Log Data**: IP address, app version, access times

### Information from Third Parties
- **Authentication**: We use Supabase for authentication
- **Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain our services
- Process your petitions and votes
- Send notifications about your activity
- Improve our platform
- Detect and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:
- **Service Providers**: Supabase (hosting), Expo (app platform)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law
- **Business Transfers**: In case of merger or acquisition

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account
- Export your data
- Opt-out of notifications
- Object to data processing

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Secure authentication (JWT tokens)
- Row-Level Security on database
- Regular security audits

## Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained while account is active
- **Petitions & Comments**: Retained indefinitely (public content)
- **Logs**: Retained for 90 days

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify you of changes via email or app notification.
